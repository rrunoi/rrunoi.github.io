About

Originally from the United States I now live with my family as a permanent resident in Finland. I am currently a Business Information Technology student at Haaga-Helia with an emphasis on software development. With the experience I have mostly being on the frontend I would like start in a frontend position, but I would like to learn more about both front and backend. I am an easy-going person who can work in a group or by myself. I have a beginner level understanding of Finnish and I am looking to improve this as well.

During my studies I have worked as part of a scrum team on two occasions. During the first of these projects, we worked in two week sprints. For this project I started on a team working with database creation and setup. Upon completion of the database work the members moved to other teams, and I was moved to the frontend. While on the frontend team I worked with creating charts the user could select the types of data they wanted in the chart or graph and that data would be pulled from the database and put in the chart. I also worked with the team who was responsible for setting up the page theme. We selected colors and the general page layout. The second project I worked on I was on the IoT team. This team was responsible for getting the PIR sensor to work with the esp32 board and send messages to the backend using an MQTT broker. The DB administrator was also able to ping each board to ensure they were working if a signal had not been sent for a while.
